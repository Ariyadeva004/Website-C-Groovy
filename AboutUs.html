About C’Groovy

C’Groovy is a vibrant music platform built to bring the best of Pop, R&B, and K-Pop to passionate listeners around the world. What began as a grassroots idea in a university dorm room has grown into a globally recognized name in digital music streaming and curation.

2024 - The Idea Was Born

Founded by music enthusiasts and developers during at Binus University project, C’Groovy began as a simple platform to share curated playlists.

2024 - Initial Launch

The first version of C’Groovy was launched, offering basic streaming features and a growing community of music fans.

2024 - Major Revamp

A full redesign introduced genre-based browsing, artist profiles, and expanded our song database significantly.

2025 - International Expansion

C’Groovy went global with multi-language support and cross-border licensing agreements, reaching audiences in over 40 countries.
